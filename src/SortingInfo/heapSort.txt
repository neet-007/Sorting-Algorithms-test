Pseudo Code:!
1- Build a max heap from the input array.
2- Swap the root of the heap with the last element of the heap.
3- Reduce the size of the heap by one and heapify the root.
4- Repeat until the heap size is 1.
---
Information:!
Memory: O(1).
Time: O(n log n).
Type: Comparison-based, In-place.
Best Use Case: When you need a guaranteed O(n log n) time complexity and in-place sorting.
